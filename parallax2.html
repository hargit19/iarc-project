ABOUT IARC

The Institute Alumni Relations Cell is a student body working under the aegis of the Dean of Resources and Alumni Affairs (DORA), IIT Roorkee. The vision of the cell is to connect our alumni to their alma mater and foster the alumni network through various initiatives, including Class Song'2018, Raionzo, which a one of a kind student alumni fest, Alumni Career Talks (ACT), where we invite our esteemed alumni to guide the students on various topics, R-Land memoirs, where we take a glimpse on IIT Roorkee's rich history, etc. IARC is also working on the alum database under DORA's guidance and aims to provide Alumni mentorship to current students.The IARC has started its work on all of its initiatives and has received positive responses and appreciation. IARC hopes to introduce new initiatives and programs with your help and support in the forthcoming years to provide mentorship to current students.
